# 1. Project Identity

## Purpose

EPSM Manager OPS is the operational module of the EPSM ecosystem.

Its purpose is to provide a reliable, scalable and business-oriented production management system for È Pronto Si Mangia.

OPS is responsible for recording production activities while preserving historical integrity and providing high-quality operational data for future analytical modules.

Analytics, reporting and KPI visualization are intentionally developed as independent modules.
