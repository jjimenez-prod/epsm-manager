# Roadmap

Project: EPSM Ecosystem

Primary Product: EPSM Manager OPS

Organization: È Pronto Si Mangia

Version: 2.0

Status: Active

Last Updated: July 2026

---

# 1. Purpose

This roadmap defines the planned evolution of the EPSM ecosystem.

Its purpose is to describe the strategic direction of the platform while allowing incremental and controlled development.

The roadmap is intentionally flexible.

Business priorities may evolve over time.

New opportunities may appear.

However, every evolution should preserve the architectural principles, business rules and long-term vision of the platform.

The roadmap describes where the platform is expected to go.

It does not replace the project backlog.

---

# 2. Roadmap Philosophy

EPSM evolves through continuous business capabilities rather than isolated software features.

Development follows an incremental approach where each released version delivers meaningful business value.

The platform grows by extending existing capabilities instead of redesigning completed work.

Roadmap priorities may change.

Architecture should remain stable.

Business Rules should remain stable.

Project Principles should remain stable.

The roadmap evolves.

The foundations do not.

---

# 5. Platform Evolution

The EPSM ecosystem evolves through successive business capabilities.

Each phase delivers a complete operational improvement while preserving the stability of existing modules.

---

## Phase 1 — Operational Foundation

Status

Completed

Primary Module

EPSM Manager OPS

Objective

Replace manual production registration with a reliable web-based operational platform.

Major Deliverables

- Production registration
- Dough Batch management
- Recipe selection
- Operator management
- Product registration
- Remaining Dough management
- Production editing
- Production history
- Operational validation
- GitHub Pages deployment
- Supabase backend
- Documentation framework

Business Outcome

Production information becomes centralized, traceable and permanently stored.

OPS becomes the operational entry point of the EPSM ecosystem.

---

## Phase 2 — Business Analytics

Status

In Progress

Primary Module

EPSM Manager Analytics

Objective

Transform operational data into actionable business information.

Major Deliverables

- Executive Dashboard
- Daily Production Overview
- KPI Dashboard
- Historical Analysis
- Production Comparison
- Operator Performance
- Business Trends
- Production Detail
- Dashboard Filtering
- Responsive Desktop Experience

Business Outcome

Managers gain immediate visibility into production performance without depending on spreadsheets.

Analytics consumes operational data while remaining a read-only module.

# 8. Long-Term Success

The EPSM ecosystem will be considered successful when:

- Production is fully digital.
- Operational information is centralized.
- Business decisions are supported by reliable data.
- Dashboards replace manual spreadsheet analysis.
- Historical information remains trustworthy.
- New modules can be introduced without redesigning existing architecture.
- Documentation evolves together with the software.
- The platform remains simple to operate despite growing functionality.

Success is measured by business value rather than by the amount of implemented software.

---

# 9. Roadmap Governance

The roadmap represents strategic direction rather than a fixed implementation schedule.

Business priorities may evolve.

New opportunities may emerge.

Existing priorities may be reordered.

Every roadmap modification should:

- Be supported by a business need.
- Respect the architectural principles of EPSM.
- Preserve compatibility with existing modules.
- Be documented before implementation.

The roadmap should remain ambitious while staying realistic.

---

# 10. Final Statement

The EPSM roadmap represents the long-term evolution of the ecosystem.

It provides strategic direction without restricting future innovation.

The platform is intentionally designed to evolve through independent modules, reusable business capabilities and stable architectural foundations.

Technologies may change.

Business priorities may evolve.

Implementation order may change.

However, the long-term objective remains constant:

To build a simple, reliable and scalable business platform that continuously improves the operational management of È Pronto Si Mangia while preserving historical integrity, architectural consistency and long-term maintainability.

The roadmap evolves.

The vision remains.

The architecture remains.

The business remains.
