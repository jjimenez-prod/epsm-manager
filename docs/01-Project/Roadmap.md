# Roadmap

**Project:** EPSM Manager

**Client:** È Pronto Si Mangia

**Version:** 1.0

**Status:** Approved

**Last Update:** 2026-07-02

---

# 1. Purpose

This roadmap defines the planned evolution of EPSM Manager.

It represents the long-term vision of the platform while allowing iterative and controlled development.

The roadmap may evolve over time, but every change must be documented and approved.

---

# 2. Product Vision

EPSM Manager will evolve from a simple production registration tool into the central operational platform of È Pronto Si Mangia.

The objective is to digitalize operational processes while maintaining simplicity, reliability and scalability.

---

# 3. Development Strategy

Development will follow an incremental approach.

Each Sprint must produce a usable improvement.

No Sprint should introduce unnecessary complexity.

---

# 7. Success Indicators

The project will be considered successful when:

- Production registration is fully digital.
- Manual spreadsheets are no longer required for data entry.
- Operational KPIs are automatically generated.
- Dashboard information is available through the web platform.
- Operational decisions are based on reliable data.
- The platform remains scalable and maintainable.

---

# 8. Project Philosophy

The roadmap is intentionally evolutionary.

Small improvements delivered continuously are preferred over large disruptive releases.

The platform should grow organically while preserving simplicity and operational stability.
